About Us

Eventismo is an Event and Entertainment company based in Nairobi Kenya and established in 2015. We also take pride in catering to all kinds of events such as conferences, seminars, award ceremonies, gala dinners, product launches, cultural events, fashion shows, exhibition stands, private events, corporate gatherings, wedding planners, team building, a Trade Fairs, Concerts, fun day, Master of ceremony (MC) and more.
