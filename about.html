Company Profile
Follow us -

Eventismo is an Event and Entertainment company based in Nairobi Kenya and established in 2015. Our main goal is to create experiences that not only reach your expectations but go way beyond to the routes. What we do is that we provide a powerful integrated in-house service portfolio to our clients. We also take pride in catering to all kinds of events such as conferences, seminars, award ceremonies, gala dinners, product launches, cultural events, fashion shows, exhibition stands, private events, corporate gatherings, wedding planners, team building, a Trade Fairs, Concerts, fun day, Master of ceremony (MC) and more

We are fully equipped to produce a wide range of events from conception to completion. Eventismo, driven by passion and big ideas, provides customized and strategic event experiences where guests connect with our clients’ brand and products in ways that are personally relevant and memorable. Our approach is mainly focused on the customer’s needs and the quality of service. Our team offers creative vision, professionalism, and expertise to create the best experience with constant care, attention to detail, originality and more than satisfying results. Always prepared and set to deliver required services to clients and delivering the solutions that shape up success to fit into the desired business plans. Diversified with thoughts and ideas, the goal stays common, to achieve and deliver SUCCESS. Delivering cost effective innovative solutions is what we are committed to. Achieving highest level of customer satisfaction becomes the key ingredient of our growth.
